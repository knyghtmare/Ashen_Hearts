ASHEN HEARTS AND DWARVEN KINGDOM TIMELINE
=====================================

** 340 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- The Golden Kingdom of the dwarves is founded to the northwest of Knalga under the leadership of King Dhundurum.

** 350-389 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- The Golden Kingdom decides to emancipate itself from the rest of Knalga, becoming its own independent Dwarven nation.
- Trade relations between the Golden Kingdom and Knalga are stringent but not non-existent.

** 440 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- King Dhundurum passes away due to old age after reigning for an entire century.
- Crown Prince Dhuranthum, eldest son of King Dhundurum, is crowned as the new King.

** 460-500 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- Dwarves of the Golden Kingdom begin researching automation technology.
- Prototypes of battle-ready clockwork automata are stationed in their fortresses and cities.

** 500 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- King Dhuranthum is killed in battle against the mighty trolls of the Fungal Undergrowths.
- Crown Prince Thurandrim, son of King Dhuranthum, is crowned as the next King of the Golden Kingdom.
- Trolls of the Fungal Undergrowths are decisively conquered in retaliative military might by the Golden Kingdom Dwarves.

** 534-535 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- The events of the campaign **Northern Rebirth** take place.
- The **Northern Alliance** is established.

** 540 - 548 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- Emissaries from the Northern Alliance arrive from Dwarven Doors and Knalga to request the Golden Kingdom to come under the banners of the Northern Alliance.
- King Thurandrim rejects the alliance, misinterpreting the invitation as a hostile takeover.
- Frequent skirmishes between the Northern Alliance and Golden Kingdom take place.


** 550 - 551 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- Lord Hamel of Knalga sends an expedition to Kal Kartha to determine the fate of the Hammer of Thursagan. The events of the campaign **The Hammer of Thursagan** take place within this time period.

** 580 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- King Thurandrim abdicates the throne in favor of researching more advanced engineering feats.
- Crown Prince Thorandrum is crowned as the next King of the Golden Dwarves.

** 590 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- Ilrandh, son of King Thorandrum, is born.

** 620 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- Prince Ilrandh of the Golden Kingdom is bestowed a series of tasks to prove his worth as the Crown Prince to the Throne. **Dwarven Kingdom (DK)** begins.

** 635 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- The territory between the Ashen's Maw and the Earth's Crucible is lost from drake control due to persistent skirmishes between dwarves, merfolk, trolls and other Northern factions.

** 637 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- Menor IV ascends to the Ashen Throne, ousting Menor III in their ritualistic duel.

** 655 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- King Thorandrum of the Dwarven Kingdom is defeated in an attempt to conquer the Ashen's Maw.
- Menor IV claims his head piece jewel, the magical Oaken's Stone, as a war trophy.

** 656 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- King Ilrandh ascends to the throne of the Dwarven Kingdom.
- King Ilrandh conspires with the dwarven and merfolk advisors on how to conquer the drakes.

** 660 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- King Ilrandh marches on to the Earth's Crucible with his formidable army of dwarven veterans.
- The drakes of the Earth's Crucible are either slain or encased in petrification runes.
- King Ilrandh uses the runic magicks of frostfire to seal Khazran in rune-imbued unmelting ice in the inner chamber of the Earth's Crucible.
- **Dwarven Kingdom (DK)** ends.

** 661-669 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- The Ashen Drakes slowly begin suffering from a mysterious illness that suppresses their inner flames, can causes development of ash.

** 673-674 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- Events of **Ashen Hearts (AH)** take place.
- Herkarth becomes Menor V

** 675 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- Defying all pre-established gender-bias, Princess Ylandra is crowned Queen of the Golden Kingdom.
- In order to recover from the resounding defeat at the Ashen's Maw, Queen Ylandra has the gates to the Golden Kingdom closed off until their military might replenishes.

** 676-740 YW **
===================
'''Location: Northlands, The Great Continent'''

- The Drakes of the Ashen's Maw begin expanding their territories.
- The Drakes become the dominant faction of the Heartfang Mountains.

** Sources **
===================
- Ashen Hearts (AH)
- Dwarven Kingdom (DK)

* Dwarven Kingdom is a complementary prequel to Ashen Hearts that will focus on Ilrandh as he proves himself to his father, to be considered a worthy heir to the throne.
